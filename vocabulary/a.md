abandon
=======

vt. 丢弃；放弃
> ≠desert

abrupt
======

* a. 突然地，意外的

* （行为）粗鲁的，唐突的

aback
=====

adv. 吓了一跳

be taken aback 被吓了一跳

asset
=====

* n. 优点，长处
    > i.e. A sense of humour is a great asset.

* (pl.)资产

    > ≈belongs, possessions; ≠liability

    > i.e. fixed assets 固定资产； liquid assets 流动资产
